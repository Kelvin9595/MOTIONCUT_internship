About Us

Welcome to CARPOOL, your trusted destination for buying and selling high-quality second-hand cars across the vast and diverse landscape of India. We understand that finding the perfect pre-owned vehicle can be a challenging endeavor, but we are here to simplify the process and provide you with a seamless and transparent experience. At CARPOOL, we have a passion for automobiles and a commitment to delivering value to both buyers and sellers. With our extensive network of dealers, individual sellers, and a robust online platform, we aim to bridge the gap between those looking for a reliable used car and those looking to find new homes for their trusted vehicles.

Our Vision
Our vision is to revolutionize the way people buy and sell used cars in India. We envision a future where every transaction is marked by trust, convenience, and satisfaction. We strive to become the go-to destination for all your second-hand car needs, catering to the diverse tastes and requirements of customers from every corner of this incredible nation.

Why Choose CARPOOL?
Wide Selection: We offer a diverse range of well-maintained, quality-tested used cars from all major manufacturers. Whether you're looking for a compact city car, a rugged SUV, or a luxurious sedan, you'll find options that suit your preferences and budget.
Trusted Sellers: We partner with certified dealers and individual sellers who meet strict quality and transparency standards. This means that you can shop with confidence, knowing that the vehicle's history and condition have been thoroughly inspected.
Nationwide Reach: We have a strong presence across India, with listings from all states and union territories. No matter where you are, you can find the perfect vehicle with CARPOOL.
Transparency: We believe in transparency throughout the buying and selling process. Our listings provide detailed information about each car, including price, mileage, maintenance history, and high-quality photos. We also facilitate easy communication between buyers and sellers, ensuring that you have all the information you need to make an informed decision.
Buyer and Seller Support: Our dedicated support team is here to assist you at every step of the process. Whether you're a first-time buyer or a seasoned seller, we offer guidance and assistance to make your experience as smooth as possible.


Our Commitment CARPOOL is not just a marketplace;
it's a commitment to building a community of satisfied car enthusiasts who understand the value of a reliable, well-maintained pre-owned vehicle. We are dedicated to promoting sustainability by extending the life of cars and ensuring they find new owners who will cherish them.
We invite you to explore our extensive listings, connect with our trusted sellers, and experience the ease and satisfaction of buying and selling second-hand cars in India with CARPOOL. Join us in this exciting journey towards a brighter automotive future.
Thank you for choosing CARPOOL, where trust and quality meet on the road to your next adventure.
